Frequently asked Question

Learn about OneDosh from FAQ!

OneDosh is a digital neobank that lets you open a multi‑currency account in minutes, send and receive money globally, exchange currencies at competitive rates, and even hold funds in fiat or stablecoins—all in one seamless ecosystem.
Currently development is on going, but our MVP is projected to be released on the second week of July, 2025.
OneDosh currently supports major fiat currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, NGN, etc.) as well as popular stablecoins (USDC, USDT). You can seamlessly convert between them with zero fees and market‑leading FX rates
You can sign up in under 3 minutes via our mobile app. You’ll need: A valid government‑issued ID (passport or national ID), Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement, dated within the last 3 months), A selfie for identity verification. Once verified, your multi‑currency wallet is ready to use.
